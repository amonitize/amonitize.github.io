Personality
Kyosuke is an intellectual who specializes in strategy. Though he always supports Yusuke's freewheeling, and even follows him up from time to time, his own talent is unparalleled. He sometimes worries too much about his older brother, and can be a bit overprotective of him. There are times where he doubts himself and wishes he shared his brother's confidence. However, he learns to trust in others more and rely on them after becoming an idol.
Backstory
Kyosuke is a former professional soccer player and Yusuke's younger twin brother. He blames himself for Yusuke's knee injury despite Yusuke insisting that it wasn't his fault.
