&6allein (and-six-allein) was a unit made up of young voice actors with strong personalities.

"allein" standing for the message that each of the members will work together with their individual strengths.

The group's nickname is anroku ()

disbandment

"We have an important announcement for all the supporters of &6allein.
For 3 years since 2016, we have been challenging ourselves with a wide range of things, such as variety and music.
As a result of discussions with the members, we have decided to end our activities and disband.
We can't thank our fans enough for all the support they have given us.
We hope that you will warmly support us in our individual new start from here on.

&6allein and all staff."
